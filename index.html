Developing Blockchain and NFT Applications is
Hard, Time-Consuming and Expensive
The Web3 stack is complex
Master blockchain technology, Solidity, GraphQL, web3.js, ethers.js, hardhat, node, IPFS, ERC721, ERC1155, etc. This takes a lot of time and requires a team to develop.
Infra is hard to manage & expensive
Teams would waste nearly 50% of engineering resources managing nodes, developing indexers, guaranteeing service stability ,etc. The node needs to be continuously upgraded with the upgrades of the program as they try to reinvent the wheel.
Lower R&D costs is the keys to the project
If you spend a lot of time on conceptual technology learning and infrastructure building, users will be taken away by other products and platforms.
Slow go-to-market is the biggest risk
The Web3 field is faster development than the Internet, but using heavily rate-limited, slow and incomplete APIs slow you down in an extremely competitive market, leading tolosing users and revenue.
